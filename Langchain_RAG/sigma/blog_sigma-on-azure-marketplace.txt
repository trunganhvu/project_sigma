Sigma Now On Azure Marketplace: Make Your Data Work For Everyone
NOVEMBER 7, 2024
Powerful analytics are just a few clicks away — Sigma is officially available in the Microsoft Azure Marketplace. 
Now, Azure users can unlock the full potential of Sigma’s cloud-native analytics on Azure to empower every team, technical or not, to make data-driven decisions. 
Our spreadsheet-like interface means you don’t need specialized SQL skills to dive in, ask questions, or get answers. Whether you’re a data analyst or a business user, Sigma gives you the power to work with your data intuitively and collaboratively. And best of all, you can leverage your existing Azure commitments to get started quickly.
Why Sigma on Azure?
Today’s organizations are data-rich but insight-poor — Sigma on Azure changes that. With Sigma, anyone in your organization, from your most technical team members to business users, can explore, analyze, and visualize data directly in the cloud, with no coding required. Our spreadsheet-like interface makes querying data as easy as working in a familiar grid format, opening up insights for everyone, not just data experts.
With Sigma on Azure, your teams can:
Leverage cloud-native analytics at scale: Deploy Sigma on Azure with the speed and scale that modern organizations demand.
Maximize your Azure investments: Use your existing Azure budget to streamline procurement and get Sigma through the Marketplace.
Stay secure and compliant: Sigma meets Azure’s rigorous security standards, so you can trust your data is protected.
Accelerate time to insights: Gain meaningful insights faster with Sigma’s intuitive interface, backed by Azure’s cloud capabilities.
Key benefits for Azure customers
Sigma on Azure is built for scale, accessibility, and ease of use.  Here’s what you can look forward to:
Effortless deployment: Integrate Sigma into your Azure environment with just a few clicks and start uncovering insights.
No-code exploration: Sigma’s spreadsheet-like interface enables non-technical users to explore and visualize data, empowering teams across functions.
Unmatched scalability: As your data and business expands, Sigma’s architecture ensures seamless performance, even with massive datasets.
Get started today
Sigma is now available in the Microsoft Azure Marketplace. Azure customers can deploy Sigma instantly, making powerful analytics accessible and accelerating insights for teams. Transform your data, make the most of your Azure spend, and see how Sigma can help drive faster, smarter decisions.
To learn more, visit our Azure Marketplace listing, spin up a free trial, or contact us at marketplace@sigmacomputing.com.
