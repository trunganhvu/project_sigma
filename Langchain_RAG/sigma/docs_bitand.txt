BitAnd
Suggest Edits
Returns the bitwise AND of two numbers.
Usage
BitAnd(number1, number2)
number1 [required] A decimal number greater than or equal to 0.
number2 [required] A decimal number greater than or equal to 0.
Example
(1) Returns 0.
BitAnd(2, 4)
(2) Returns 4.
