Search in Sigma
Suggest Edits
Sigma users can use Search to locate items in their Sigma organization, including:
Workbooks
Datasets
Data stores
Templates
Folders
Tables
Sigma orders the search results by the most recently viewed by the searcher.
To access search, click the Search icon at the top left of the title bar.
If you enter a term and then press Return, the Search page opens. 
The Search page lists the matches, and lets you filter the results by document type, badge type, and owner.
