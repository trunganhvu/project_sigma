BitOr
Suggest Edits
Returns the bitwise OR of two numbers.
Usage
BitOr(number1, number2)
number1 [required] A decimal number greater than or equal to 0.
number2 [required] A decimal number greater than or equal to 0.
Example
(1) Returns 6
BitOr(2, 4)
(2) Returns 31.
