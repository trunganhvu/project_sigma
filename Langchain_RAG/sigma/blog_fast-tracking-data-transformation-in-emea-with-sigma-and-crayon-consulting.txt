Fast-Tracking Data Transformation In EMEA With Sigma And Crayon Consulting
JANUARY 28, 2025
We’re proud to announce a strategic partnership with Crayon Consulting, marking an exciting milestone as Sigma expands its footprint in the EMEA region. This collaboration brings Sigma closer to organizations in the Nordics, with Crayon's deep expertise ensuring faster, more cost-efficient analytics, AI, and change management. Together, we empower organizations to unlock new use cases, drive innovation, and accelerate their data strategy implementation.
Crayon Consulting: A Trusted Partner for Fast, Efficient Sigma Deployments
Headquartered in Oslo, with offices across Norway and Sweden, Crayon Consulting is a leader in AI, data analytics, and digital transformation. With over 300 successful projects since 2015, Crayon brings unparalleled experience in helping businesses harness data to fuel growth. Now, as a trusted Sigma partner, Crayon offers end-to-end services—from strategy and consulting to implementation and change management—designed to optimize every stage of the data journey.
What This Means for Organizations Across the Nordics and EMEA
This partnership brings Sigma closer to businesses across the Nordics, empowering them with tools that streamline data analytics, accelerate decision-making, and drive measurable ROI.

With Crayon’s regional expertise, businesses can:.
Achieve Faster Time to Value
Crayon’s deep expertise ensures that Sigma is deployed quickly, allowing organizations to start deriving value from their data almost immediately. Instead of waiting months for full implementation, businesses can begin uncovering insights within days—speeding up decision-making and enhancing efficiency.
Lower Costs Without Sacrificing Value
Leveraging Crayon’s local expertise, businesses can optimize costs while maximizing value from their data. By deploying Sigma’s self-service analytics platform, teams reduce their reliance on manual processes, cutting down on inefficiencies and lowering operational costs.
Innovate at Speed
With Sigma’s intuitive interface and Crayon’s expertise in AI and data strategy, businesses can explore new use cases, in analytics and data apps (purpose built solutions) faster. From real-time financial planning to sales forecasting, this collaboration helps companies stay ahead of market trends, driving faster decision-making and enabling agile business growth.
Ensure Change Adoption
The right tools are just part of the equation. Crayon’s change management expertise ensures that employees understand, adopt, and maximize new technologies. This holistic approach to technology adoption fosters mastery and ensures that investments in Sigma for AI and analytics use cases lead to lasting business value.
Enjoy Tailored Regional Solutions
Crayon Consulting’s deep regional knowledge ensures Sigma is deployed to meet the specific needs of Nordic businesses, including governance requirements. This expertise maximizes Sigma’s capabilities, driving efficient and impactful results across industries.
A Cost-Saving Solution Tailored for EMEA
This partnership is more than just a collaboration—it’s a commitment to empowering businesses with tools and expertise that unlock their full potential. By combining Sigma’s intuitive platform with Crayon’s deep knowledge, organizations can accelerate their data strategies, improve decision-making, and drive lasting results.
