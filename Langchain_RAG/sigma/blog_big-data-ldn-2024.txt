Big Data LDN 2024: Highlights And Insights From Team Sigma
OCTOBER 15, 2024
Big Data LDN 2024 brought together data leaders, innovators, and disruptors to tackle the biggest challenges in data, analytics, and AI. Sigma was front and center at the UK’s premier data conference, showcasing how our platform redefines what’s possible for businesses. 
We’ve captured the best moments for you. Check out these exclusive highlights where Sigma experts break down what it takes to build a truly data-driven business.
‍
Elevating Business Users with AI and the Cloud — CEO, Mike Palmer
At Big Data LDN, Mike cut straight to the point: How can businesses bridge the gap between powerful data infrastructures and everyday users? Sigma makes complex data instantly accessible, transforming everyday spreadsheet skills into a superpower for business users. Watch Mike’s video to find out how Sigma is simplifying AI for the everyday user and enabling faster decision-making.
‍
Real-Time Collaboration, No Extracts Needed – Product Evangelist, Luke Stanke
Luke laid out what sets Sigma apart from the competition. Unlike other platforms that rely on data extracts, Sigma connects directly to live cloud data, giving teams real-time insights and eliminating stale data issues. With our familiar spreadsheet-like interface, Sigma delivers the power of the cloud without the complexity of traditional BI platforms. Sigma allows you to securely write back and automate. It’s the only BI platform that lets you enter data directly into your analysis, so you can add context to your work and automate actions. Need to collaborate? Sigma makes it easy to work together in real time, adding new data or analysis on the fly. Discover how Sigma turns complex workflows into a seamless experience in Luke’s video, and see why businesses love how fast they can get up and running with us.
‍
Sigma’s European Expansion – VP of EMEA, Stuart Simmons
Sigma’s rapid expansion into Europe is one of the most exciting stories of Big Data LDN. Stuart explains why Sigma is the only analytics platform truly built for the cloud, and how we’re helping European businesses unlock the power of their cloud data warehouses. From secure, real-time analytics to the ability to write back to data, Sigma offers capabilities no other platform can match. Check out Stuart’s full video to see why the buzz is building around Sigma across the globe - and PS. he is hiring!
‍
The Future of Data Apps – VP of Solution Engineering, Alfonso Franklin
Alfonso laid out the future of analytics, outlining how Sigma is evolving beyond traditional BI and pushing it forward with data applications. He walked through key use cases, like supply chain optimization and scenario modeling, showing how Sigma blends AI, data, and analytics in ways other platforms can’t. With write-back capabilities and real-time data access, Sigma is more than a BI tool—it’s a launchpad for actionable insights. Watch Alfonso’s interview to understand how Sigma is setting a new standard for the industry.
