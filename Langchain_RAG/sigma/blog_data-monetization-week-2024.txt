Data Monetization Week 2024 at Sigma: Unlocking Value from Your Data
Welcome to Data Monetization Week 2024! This week is dedicated to exploring the strategies and tools for extracting more value from data, as well as highlighting best practices and case studies that demonstrate how forward-thinking businesses leverage their data for revenue generation. 
Data isn't just information—it's a goldmine waiting to be explored. Whether at the core of your product or as a premium analytics experience, your organization can leverage embedded experiences to unlock new revenue streams. Throughout this week, we’ll share invaluable insights and real-world examples to guide you on your data monetization journey.
Ahead this week
Monday: From Zero to Data App
Kick off the week with a comprehensive training session hosted by Zalak Trivedi, Sigma Product Manager, and Ioana Munteanu, Sigma Software Engineer. Learn how to go "From Zero to Data App" and build powerful data applications that can transform your business operations.
‍
Tuesday: Fly Customers to the Moon—How to Build Engaging Embedded Analytics Applications
Join Will Wood, VP Data Solutions at Brooklyn Data, and Zalak Trivedi, Sigma Product Manager, for an insightful training on building engaging embedded analytics applications. Learn how to enhance customer experiences and drive engagement with cutting-edge embedded analytics.
Wednesday: Invest Smarter: Monetizing Data with Sigma & Where to Invest
In this captivating customer interview, Tapan Trivedi, CEO of Where to Invest, shares his journey of leveraging data to generate revenue. Hosted by Sigma’s Zalak Trivedi, this session will provide real-world insights into the transformative power of data monetization.
Thursday: How Mindbody Built White-Labeled Embedded Analytics at Scale
Explore a compelling customer story on how Mindbody used data applications to drive their data monetization strategy. This session will highlight practical applications and the impact of data-driven decisions on business success. Learn more about the fundamentals of data applications.
Do More with Your Data
Don't miss out on the opportunity to elevate your data strategy. Explore the event schedule, participate in our sessions, and start your Sigma journey with a free trial. Join us for Data Monetization Week 2024 and unlock the full potential of your data!
